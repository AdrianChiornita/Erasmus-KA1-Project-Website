This was the arrival day and from the beginning we could experience some amazing views during the flight.

Greetings from Porto!

Today we had such good times in Porto, where we visited the Cathedral of Porto and train station of Porto. Of course we didn’t forget to visit the wine reserves from Offley where we tasted some great white wine.

More amazing sights!

Today we visited the Castle of Guimaraes where we tasted some Portugese history. Furthermore, we saw the great sights from Bom Jesus, where everything was perfect, from the cathedral, to the endless stairs and sights.

Something new

Today we started working on this AWESOME page in the Indot building, so this is where all the fun began; just look at the site.

Hello there, Spain!

Today we saw the cathedral from Santiago De Compostela from Spain, and even though the weather was horrible, the sights outweighed it.

The last trip

Today was a sad day for all of us as this was our last excursion, but we saw the beautiful Ponte de Lima and Santa Lucia, which is surrounded by the most amazing sights ever. And last but not least, we saw the ocean.
