Portugal- Barcelos

Portugal, officially the Portuguese Republic is a country in southwest Europe. It is located on the Iberian Peninsula, and it is the westernmost country of mainland Europe, being bordered by the Atlantic Ocean to the west and south and by Spain to the north and east.


Fact: Portuguese language is spoken by about 230 million people around the world (including 210 native speakers), and is the official language of 9 countries.
